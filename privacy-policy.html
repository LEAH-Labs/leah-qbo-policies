Privacy Policy - LEAH Labs Invoice Automation

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

Overview

LEAH Labs Invoice Automation is an internal business application designed exclusively for LEAH Labs' accounting automation. This application connects to QuickBooks Online to streamline invoice processing and transaction matching.

Data Collection and Use

This application accesses QuickBooks Online data solely for internal accounting automation purposes. The application:

  • Reads transaction and vendor data from your QuickBooks account
  • Uploads invoice attachments to match with existing transactions
  • Does not store QuickBooks data outside of the QuickBooks system
  • Does not share any data with third parties

Data Security

All data access occurs through secure QuickBooks APIs using OAuth 2.0 authentication. No QuickBooks data is permanently stored by this application.

Internal Use Only

This application is for internal use by LEAH Labs only and is not distributed to other companies or individuals.
